Simulates forces between particles to create lifelike shapes. Note that the forces do not follow the laws of physics.

Particle interactions:

  • The red particles are "mellow" and clump together
  • The green particles repel each other but are attracted to the blue and red particles
  • The blue particles are "energetic" and are repelled by red and green particles
