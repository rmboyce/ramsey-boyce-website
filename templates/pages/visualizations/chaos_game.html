Starting with a point inside a polygon, the program repeatedly plots a new point a fraction of the distance toward a randomly chosen vertex. Over time, these points form a fractal pattern.

The "Sierpinski Fraction" is the size ratio of non-overlapping sub-polygons packed within the main polygon. Calculated via `CalculateCarpetFraction()`, this ratio generates clean fractal designs.
